Inca Expert

Just over 6 years ago, I was tasked with creating, designing, and developing a new travel brand, focusing on a more exclusive audience, a business with an entirely digital sales process. Now Inca Expert is one of the highest-rated travel agencies in the world according to Trustpilot. Currently, it is a company with annual revenues of $7M+, over 50 employees, serving 1000+ passengers per year, and with offices in Peru and the USA.

From the beginning, I was responsible for setting the tone, structure, and design of the website.
Website performance improvements

Creating a website is always the first step, and perseverance and continuous improvement are key to building not just websites but digital businesses that stand the test of time.

From the outset, reviewing metrics and conducting A/B tests have been essential to create a beautiful, efficient site with high conversion rates. This approach has allowed us to enhance the performance of CTAs, improve form conversion, optimize mobile versions, change the structure of our pages, and simplify many elements of our websites.

Internal Projects

Our area consistently embark on additional projects that contribute to various aspects of our agency's operations. One project involved the collaboration with other areas such as the Sales Intelligence team for design the "Biblio" system, an internal hotel search system.

This system enables us to gather pertinent information about the numerous hotels we collaborate with, serving as a valuable reference for our sales team. It ensures that our sales people have all the necessary information readily available to facilitate customer consultations.

Newsletters

I oversee the newsletters, a crucial communication channel with a database of approximately 10,000 individuals.

We implement A/B testing here as well to enhance our delivery performance. This effort has led to a twofold increase in conversions through this medium in less than a year.
